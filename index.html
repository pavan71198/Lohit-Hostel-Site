Welcome to Lohit Hostel

Inaugurated in 2014, Lohit is the 10th boysâ€™ hostel in the campus. This hostel houses UGs and PGs. The hostel has a reading room, table tennis room, television room and music room to ensure a pleasant stay to all the boarders. In addition, the hostel has a juice centre, canteen and stationery shop to cater the need of the students.




Greetings to all the students of Lohit Hostel, one of the young ones in the hostel fraternity of IIT Guwahati!
Our hostel is still in the construction and development stage with the coming up of new blocks D and E, adding to the already existing A, B and C blocks. Currently, we have a formidable strength of 725 students with the target of reaching the full capacity of 1,050 shortly.
The extremely dedicated Hostel Management Committee members are working hard to provide you with a desirable ambience for academic, sports and various culturalactivities. As it happens in the development stage, a lot of facilities are being added to the hostel slowly. We request you to be patient and be part of the journey of making Lohit hostel the best hostel in IIT Guwahati. We look forward to your whole-hearted participation and co-operation in this regard. We wish you all the best for a cherishable stay in Lohit hostel.

Warden and Associate Wardens,
Lohit hostel.
