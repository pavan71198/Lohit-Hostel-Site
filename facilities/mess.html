Lohit Hostel Mess

Mess Information

Lohit Mess has newly established kitchen equipped with hot air exhausting chimney and air conditioning system in cooking area. The mess is designed to serve the food to eleven hundred students whereas currently approximately eight hundred students are taking food in mess. The mess includes thirty-four working staff. The workers of the mess wear hand-glows, cap, and their assigned uniform on daily basis. The MMC (Mess management Committee) member look after the hygiene in mess and kitchen premise, quality of food, punctuality of mess time, preparation of menu and other issues related to mess. For the preparation of mess menu online suggestion via google form has been taken from boarders on monthly basis.On the basis of received suggestion and discussion among MMC member and Caterer the menu will be prepared. The prepared menu will be sent to boarder via webmail and posted on mess notice board and hostel webpage. The daily complain and suggestion will be addressed and resolved by MMC member. Depending upon the severity of problem the action will be taken against the caterer. The action taken on such issues will be displayed on notice board of lohit mess.
